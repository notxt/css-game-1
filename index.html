You find yourself in a long hallway

Tall paintings of skeletal figures line the walls

On one side you see a cracked door. On the other you see a closed door with a bright light behind it.

The hallway continues on growing brighter and seeming to grow in size as you walk down it.

You find yourself in a dimly lit room with towering bookshelves
